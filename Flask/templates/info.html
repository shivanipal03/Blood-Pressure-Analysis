Why Monitoring Blood Pressure is Essential?

Regular blood pressure monitoring helps in:

  • Early Detection: Identifying potential issues before they escalate.
  • Effective Management: Adjusting lifestyle and treatment plans as needed.
  • Health Monitoring: Tracking changes over time for better health outcomes.
